The claim
"Drink lemon water every morning and burn 500 calories without lifting a finger."
What we found
Lemon water does not burn 500 calories. A glass contains ~6 calories and has no proven metabolism-boosting effect of that magnitude. No peer-reviewed study supports the claim.
