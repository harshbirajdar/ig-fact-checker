The claim
"Humans only use 10% of their brain — imagine what we could do if we unlocked the rest."
@mindhacks Reel · 18 sec
What we found
The "10% of brain" claim is a long-debunked myth — neuroimaging shows virtually all regions are active across a 24-hour period. However, at any given moment only a small subset of neurons fire simultaneously, which is where the myth originated.

ACCURATE
Verdict certainty: 92%
The claim
"Honey never spoils — archaeologists found edible honey in Egyptian tombs."
@kitchen.facts Post
What we found
Largely accurate. Honey's low moisture, low pH, and hydrogen peroxide content prevent microbial growth, so sealed honey can last indefinitely. Edible honey has been reported from Egyptian tombs. Caveat: crystallization is normal, and contaminated or diluted honey can spoil.
